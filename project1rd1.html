  • First Question
    • What topics of discussion, issues, problems, or concerns keep your discourse community together?

      • As for issues problems, or concerns when people's’ futures are impacted by a certain external force they also come together as an opposing force. A great example of this is how the DACA is currently being jeopardized so a lot of the ‘dreamers’ would march around the school chanting on how we should save the DACA.
    • What constitutes new knowledge for your group?
      • New knowledge can mean a multitude of different ideas. It can be anything from learning how to program to new ways of looking at current events, to even just new life skills.

      • Another way we can do this is through just learning through the institution itself.
