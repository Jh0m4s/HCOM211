Reflection on Discourses

As a whole I believe that Project 1 was pretty fun to work on. I think the word Discourse is very important to learn about. I also enjoyed the reading that went along with the class. I believe this project should be reviewed for the classes to come because it is a great paper to introduce your class with because it offers the most freedom out of any paper. It really encourages students to make an essay that they really feel passionate about.
